The Origin of Aria

Aria was established by Kirloskar Pneumatic Company Limited., a leader in air and gas compression systems, to advance next-generation screw air compressor technology. Built on decades of engineering expertise, Aria represents a focused commitment to innovation, performance, and reliability in industrial air solutions.

Global Technology Partnerships

SRM Sweden

In partnership with SRM Sweden, the pioneers of screw compressor technology, Aria integrates world-class rotor design and advanced engineering expertise. This collaboration enables high-efficiency compressors that deliver superior performance, durability, and reliability across industrial applications.

City, University of London

Our collaboration with the Centre for Compressor Technology at City, University of London brings cutting-edge research into real-world applications. From advanced rotor profiles to next-generation design methodologies, this partnership strengthens Aria’s technological edge and innovation capability.

Atmos Chrást

Through our partnership with Atmos Chrást, a leading European compressor manufacturer, Aria delivers solutions tailored for tropical operating conditions. These systems are engineered to perform reliably at high ambient temperatures while offering extended lifespan and improved efficiency.

Manufacturing Excellence

At Aria, manufacturing is driven by precision, consistency, and a commitment to continuous improvement. Our advanced facilities, combined with stringent quality control processes, ensure that every compressor meets the highest standards of performance, reliability, and durability. From design to final assembly, every stage is engineered to deliver products that perform consistently in demanding industrial environments.

Mastering Complex Castings

Our in-house casting capabilities enable the production of complex, high-integrity components that form the foundation of our compressors. With deep expertise in metallurgy, precision moulding, and quality control, we ensure each casting meets strict structural and performance requirements. This capability allows us to maintain complete control over quality, improve durability, and deliver components that perform reliably under demanding operating conditions.

Precision Machining Prowess

Aria’s advanced machining capabilities ensure the production of high-precision components with exceptional accuracy and consistency. Using state-of-the-art CNC systems, high-precision grinding, and advanced manufacturing processes, we achieve tight tolerances critical for compressor efficiency and reliability. This precision engineering enables superior performance, reduced wear, and long-term operational stability across all our products.
